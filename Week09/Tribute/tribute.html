Jerry W. Bear

September 28, 1946 - September 29, 2019

The life of Jerry Bear

Jerry Wayne Bear was a man of few words. He spent much of his life as an over the road truck driver but he was so much more than that. He was a husband, father, grandfather, and great grandfather. He was a fair man who never differentiated between his step children and biological children. He treated his step children and step grandchildren as if they were his own and in many ways they were. He was married to Wealthy A. Hutchison on October 10, 1975.

Jerry was someone to look up to. He was hard working man and who there for his family. He worked for and retired from Arch Chemicals of Brandenburg, Kentucky. He spent weeks at a time on the road but when he would return from his travels, he spent that time with his family. To grow up and be half the man that he was, would be an accomplishment.

He enjoyed hunting, fishing and loved being on the countyside. He was a man who was loved by so many. He was always there to help anyone in need. He continued to go hunting up to his passing in 2019.

Rest In Peace Jerry Wayne Bear
