Jerry w. Bear

September 28, 1946 - September 29, 2020

The life of Jerry Bear

Jerry Wayne Bear was a mann of few words. He spent much of his life as a truck driver but he was so much more than that. He was a husband, father, stepfather, grandfather, stepgrandfather, great grandfather, and great step grandfather.He was a fair man who never differentiated between his step children and biological children. He treated his step children and step grandchildren the same as if they were his own and in many ways they were.
